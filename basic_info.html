When, Where, and Why did the Munich Agreement Take Place?

Basic Information Where Link Home

When?

The Munich Agreement took place almost exactly a year before the beginning of WWII (September 1939), in September of 1938. The conference took place on the 29th shortly before 1pm, and the debate lasted 3 hours. The Agreement (document) was signed a day later on September 30th 1938


Where?

The Munich Agreeement happened in Munich, Germany. The conference, and signing took place in the Führerbau (loosely translating to Führer's building), which was used as a representative for Adolf Hitler during the Nazi period of WWII.
