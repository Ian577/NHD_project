When?

The Munich Agreement took place almost exactly a year before the beginning of WWII (September 1939), in September of 1938. The conference took place on the 29th shortly before 1pm, and the debate lasted 3 hours. The Agreement (document) was signed that day.


Where?

The Munich Agreeement happened in Munich, Germany. The conference, and signing took place in the Führerbau (loosely translating to Führer's building), which was used as a representative building for Adolf Hitler during the Nazi period of WWII.


Why?

The Munich agreement happened when United Kingdom's prime minister Neville Chamberlain rquested an imediate meeting to settle the dispute over Czechoslovakia. It was his attempt to prevent war with Germany.
