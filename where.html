Führerbau

Führerbau, which translates to Führer's building in German is a building that was used as a representative building for Adolf hitler during the Nazi period. It is located in Munich, Germany, and it was the bulding where British prime minister Neville Chamberlain, and Frence prime minister Edouard Daladier signed the Munich Pact with Adolf Hitler in 1938.



After WWII

After the war the Führerbau was used as a storage house for art that had been stolen by Nazi soldiers. Today it is the main building of the Hochschule für Musik und Theater München, which is the Munich Usiversity of Music and Preforming arts.
