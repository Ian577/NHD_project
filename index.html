Quick Facts

What was the Munich agreement?

The Munich agreement was a debate between Britain, France, Italy, and Germany. The debate, and agreement was to prevent war between the countries.

What did opposing sides of the agreement win, or lose?

The deal was that Britain would surrender the Sudetenland of Czechoslovakia to Germany, where roughly 3 mllion Germans lived. If they did not agree, they would fight for it.
