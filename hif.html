What Happened?

Only 6 months after being signed, Germany violated the terms of the agreement. They started invading all of Czechoslovakia. The agreement said they only would get the sudetenland. The violation of the agreement started the war. I think that part of the reason the agreement failed is because there was nothing enforcing the terms of the agreement.
